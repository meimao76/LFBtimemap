London Emergency Response Network

Explore real-time fire response and incidents in London 2024.

Welcome to the London Emergency Response Network visualization platform.

Based on the real fire response data of London Fire Bridge from Jan to Dec in 2024, this platform analyzes the spatial distribution of fire response time in different areas of the city, wishing to help the public have a better understanding of the fairness and efficiency of urban public safety services.

With an interactive map, you can visually explore fire incidents and fire response performance in different areas of London and understand the spatial characteristics of the city's emergency response system.
